1. When register a new user

- Check if username has the following constraints

  1. Not empty
  2. Is a valid email

- Check if password has the following constraints

  1. Not empty
  2. Minimum length of 6
  3. Maximum length of 10
  4. Has no spaces
  5. Has at least one lowercase letter
  6. Has at least one uppercase letter
  7. Has at least one digit
  8. Has at least one symbol (._-!$)

- Create a new user in the database
- Send any email to new user
